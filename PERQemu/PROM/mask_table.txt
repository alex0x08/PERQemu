This is NOT the PERQ ROM, but rather a synthesized table used to
tag each word with a mask type as it flows through the source and
destination FIFOs.  The CombinerFlags (see RasterOp.cs) identifies
each word as outside the update region, as a leading or trailing
edge, or as a full update word be taken.  It is fed into makerom.pl
to produce the rds00emu.rom image.  There should be 512 entries here.

Note: "phase" is only the two low bits of the RasterOp phase (from
the CntlRasterOp register). You are not expected to understand this.

result	00 (00000) = Invalid
	01 (00001) = DontMask
	02 (00010) = LeftEdge
	04 (00100) = RightEdge
	06 (00110) = Both
	08 (01000) = FullWord
	10 (10000) = Leftover
